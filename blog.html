Shouldn't really be posting this, but I just wanted to say I think my friends and I could be in a tight squeeze in our latest adventure. It was a crazy thrill!! Helping my two friends📝🙄

March 22nd, 1995

Ugh.. I wish my friends Harry and Ron would pay attention in class more. I keep on telling them to study. I always end up doing so well that they need my help. Those two can be complete idiots sometimes, but that's what I love about them. Thankfully Harry focuses when important test and homework are due. Unlike Ron who did not realize that we had a test in Potions. I can see why Professor Snape hated Ron with a passion now. But, I feel like there is something about Ron that I just like about him.

Dancing the Night Away!💃

November 21st, 1994

OMG!!! I had one of the best time at Hogwarts this year! Viktor Krum a professional Quidditch player asked me out to the Yule Ball. It's one of the traditions when they host the Tri-Wizard Tournament. I was truly shocked because I did not think anyone would want to ask me to the ball. Although it was fun, I had just wished a certain someone would've asked me instead. Still, I have no regrets and enjoyed myself!

Horrible Nightmare!💀🐍

April 18th, 1992

You are not going to believe me, but I was pertrified and stiff as a statue!! It felt as if I was stuck in everlasting moment just filled with my thoughts. I was so unsure about myself, it made me question if I was dreaming that the last two years were even real. It felt weird though since I could hear Harry and Ron's voices, but I couldn't answer them. I'm not quite sure how, but I think Harry and Ron saved me and I was so happy to see them and that everything till was real and not a dream.

New School!💫😅

August 1st, 1991

I am finally excited to be attending the school of wizardy and withcraft at Hogwarts!! Not sure what to expect, but one thing is absolute is that I received my acceptance letter from an OWL. YES AN ACUTAL OWL! It was mind boggling, but it is not as strange as I thought. A few weeks ago, I had a cavity and wanted my mum to take a look at it, but it DISAPPEARED! I felt like a lunatic, but magic is real! Shhh don't let Headmaster Dumbledore find out about this blog..
